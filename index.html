About Me

Hello! I'm Fares, a 20-year-old graphic designer from Tunisia with a passion for creating standout brand identities. With over four years of experience under my belt, I’ve had the opportunity to work on diverse projects, each one helping me refine my skills and deepen my understanding of design.
