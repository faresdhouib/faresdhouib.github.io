Shmoola

Brief: Shmoola is a local food delivery company. The logo design aimed to reflect speed, friendliness, and reliability.

Challenges: The challenge was to incorporate elements that represented fast service while keeping the design friendly and approachable.

Solution: The logo features a lunch box with the word 'Shmoola' written in Arabic inside it, with the use of yellow to express friendliness and dynamic lines to convey speed.
