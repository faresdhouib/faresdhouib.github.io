Brewtopia

Brief: Brewtopia is a coffee shop brand that aims to create a warm and inviting atmosphere for its customers. The brand identity needed to reflect the cozy, artisanal vibe of the shop.

Challenges: The main challenge was to design a logo that could communicate both the high-quality nature of the coffee and the relaxed ambiance of the shop.

Solution: The final logo design used earthy tones and hand-drawn elements to evoke a sense of craftsmanship and comfort, perfectly capturing the essence of Brewtopia.
